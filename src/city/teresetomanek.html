ABOUT

Duluth! I can't thank you enough for your support. Running for office is not easy. I have learned so much from all of my constituents over the last year. I have listened eagerly to the feedback that I have received on the phone and at the doors, on Council meetings, through emails, and Facebook messages. I want to thank you for trusting me, and thank you for supporting me during this campaign. I can't thank my family, and my friends who act like family enough. Thank you for knocking on doors, waving signs in cool weather, and encouraging me to keep going. To my campaign committee—thank you for your guidance and support in helping me through this journey. To the thousands of people that I met on the phone, at the doors, at events, or at Tuesday's with Terese—thank you for showing up, for your questions, and for believing in me. I am honored to serve as your city councilor, and excited to continue this important work with you! I can't wait to see what we can do together in the next four years!
